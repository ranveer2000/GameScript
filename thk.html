Thinking upon it (December 2025)

I started tinkering upon the idea of GameScript
Who were the people it could help?
The answer? Indie developers, it could easily help them
I thought I should give it a thought, try making GameScript again, started to code in C++ again
After a while I stumbled opon the idea of 'self-hosting' langauges which are langauges that compile themselves (eg. Go, C++)
I thought if I could make GameScript into a self hosting langauge
Self hosting languages work by creating a simple language compiler, then in that langauge, make a compiler for a more complex language, than in THAT compiler finally make a compiler for a fully matured langauge
The problem was, I didn't have time to do it. It was quite a long process
