What is GameScript?

GameScript is a new open-source programming language designed to make 2D game development simple and accessible.
Inspired by p5.js and Python's clean syntax, GameScript gives you built-in functions for sprites, windows, and game logic—right out of the box.
No more wrestling with overly complex languages or sacrificing speed with interpreted ones.
Currently in active development, GameScript offers the perfect blend of beginner-friendly syntax and professional-grade performance.
Whether you're just starting your game dev journey or looking for a streamlined alternative,
GameScript lets you focus on what matters: creating great games.
